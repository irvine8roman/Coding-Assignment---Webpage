Weapons!

    Swords
  • The most versatile weapon in the game, with an emphasis in burst damage over sustained DPS. Has relatively muted strengths and weaknesses.
  • Has very high utility as an offhand weapon.

    • Dual Swords
  • A quick, close range style of weapon that is easy to use.
  • Has a solid mix of damage and ki damage, with many strong defensive options

    • Spears
  • The premier poking weapon, this has the longest average range of attacks in the game, while also being high in damage.
  • This comes at the cost of Ki damage on hit.
  • It also has a wide variety of unique and powerful close range options, such as a repositioning attack that is unblockable by humans, Entangle and Spearfall, which instantly grounds humans and Skeleton Warriors, and strong mobility options in Rainbow Ruse and Flying Monkey.

    • Splitstaff
  • A high utility weapon that allows you to change the properties of your attacks by holding the button down after using them
  • Has some of the best Ki damage in the game, with the ability to attack from many different ranges and with a large amount of mobility skills.
  • This comes at the cost of raw health damage from most of your attacks.
